About Us

Developer.com is a custom software development company of 65 employees, founded in 2011. Their headquarters is in Austin, Texas, with other locations in Warsaw, Poland, Minsk, Belarus, and New York. They offer services in custom software development, and application management and support.

We developed a large, complex .NET application for a marketing agency. The client provided the blueprints, and Syberry built a multi-faceted application with a .NET backend. They also implemented numerous dependencies and third-party integrations. The client praised their high-quality work.
